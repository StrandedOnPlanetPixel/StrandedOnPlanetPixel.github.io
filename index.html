How to play

Walk to your robots to program ['Q'] them to collect resources.

If your robots have collected food and your health is low press ['E'] to eat!

Make more robots by pressing ['R'], if they all break you cant get resources.

Get to level 5 without you or your ship reaching zero health to win.
